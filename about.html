About Me:



        Hi there! :)

        My name is Giovanni, and I'm a first-generation college student from Compton, California. Currently, I am a Computer Science major at CSU Long Beach with diverse interests in software programming, mobile app development, and AI. I am also really intrigued by cybersecurity, in which I intend to pursue a minor. I'm experienced with software ranging from Adobe Photoshop to Google suite, and I am well-versed in Object Oriented Programming languages such as Java, C#, and Python. I’m also a full-stack web developer, utilizing technologies such as Node.js, Express, and Firebase.

        In the summer of 2020, I was chosen as one of fifteen students for the Inaugural Snap Engineering Academy, where I learned full-stack web development, responsive web design, and RESTful routing. A project I am really proud of is a food service web app, built for a hackathon at the Snap Engineering Academy. FoodVerse uses the Yelp API to locate restaurants near the user, providing them with photos, contact info, and ratings. The focus of this app was to filter out chain restaurants and instead highlight local businesses who might be struggling during the pandemic in order to connect hungry customers to these businesses.

        In my free time, I love to play video games and program computer games using Unity's Game Engine. I'm a huge fan of Rock music, and I like going to the beach. I've been growing out my hair since my junior year of high school, and it grows a little every day. :)
